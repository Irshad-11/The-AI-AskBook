# ONE

What is AskBook?

AskBook is the open library of high-quality AI prompts. It solves the single biggest bottleneck in the AI era: spending more time fighting the prompt than actually getting the work done.

Instead of starting from a blank screen, you leverage a global hive-mind of tested, refined instructions. Get professional-grade outputs from ChatGPT, Claude, and Midjourney—instantly.

By the community, for the community.
That’s AskBook.
